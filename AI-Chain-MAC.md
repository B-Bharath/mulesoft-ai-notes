• MuleSoft AI Chain (MAC) Connector is an AI connector for MuleSoft.
• It lets Mule apps connect to AI models (LLMs) like OpenAI, Gemini, Mistral, etc.
• You can use AI directly inside Mule flows, just like DB or HTTP connectors.
• It runs inside Anypoint Platform.

Purpose of MAC
• Make it easy to use AI in Mule applications
